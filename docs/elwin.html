About Burn to the Brim

Read about BTTB's history, open-source transition, and development team.

The original author of this program is **Elwin Oost**. As he lacked the time to continue this project, he put it out in the open under the umbrella of the GNU General Public License (GPL), making it 100% free open-source software for the community.

Today, BTTB is maintained and developed by the **BTTB Team**. For the exact list of developers and contributors working on this C++20 port and legacy versions, please see the official project summary page on SourceForge.
