Contact Page

There are different ways to contact the Burn To The Brim team:

  • For general information, you can visit the BTTB Homepage
  • By subscribing to the bttb news-list you'll hear first about new versions. This is a low volume mailing list.
  • You can discuss the program on the BTTB Forum
  • Ask for help on the help page
  • Report bugs that you have found and verified. Include all steps you have gone through to reproduce the bug.
  • Propose features you would like in Burn To The Brim. Verify these features aren't already in, in another form.
  • Finally, though we'd rather not receive general usage questions, we enjoy your comments by mail.
